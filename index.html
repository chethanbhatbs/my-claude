Install in under 20 seconds

One command. It does the rest.

No accounts, no config, no cloud. The installer sets up an isolated Python environment and opens the dashboard.

01

Paste one command

Run the line in your terminal. It downloads a small app to ~/.my-claude.

02

It sets up locally

A one-time ~20-second setup. No global installs, no admin rights.

03

Opens in your browser

Launches at 127.0.0.1:8766, reading your own ~/.claude.

Private by design

Your data never leaves your computer.

Your transcripts are sensitive. This tool treats them that way.

  • ●Binds to 127.0.0.1 only, so nobody else can reach it.
  • ●Zero external calls. No telemetry, no upload.
  • ●Reads ~/.claude on the machine it runs on. Nothing else.
  • ●Share the tool, not the data.
